📊 Plant-Level KPI Dashboard (Manufacturing Operations)

In manufacturing operations, Safety, Quality, SCM, Maintenance and Production departments track KPIs separately, causing fragmented data, manual workload and delays before Tier‑3 meetings. I built a centralized, automated Power BI dashboard integrating IoT data, SharePoint lists, Excel, and SQL sources to give leadership a real‑time single source of truth.

Business Impact: ✅ Reduced manual reporting from 2 hours to 10 seconds ✅ Improved decision-making speed for Plant Head ✅ Provided real‑time KPI visibility across all departments ✅ Eliminated data mismatch and inconsistency issues
